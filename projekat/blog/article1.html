Search Engine Optimization

Definition and usage

The process of improving quality and quantity of website traffic from search engines is called search engine optimization (SEO). It targets unpaid ("natural"/"organic") traffic rather than direct or paid traffic.


SEO considers how search engines work, the algorithms that dictate behavior of search engines, keywords and which enginers are preferred by their targeted audience. It's relevant for numerous reasons. From the user's perpective, the most important aspect is filtering irrelevant results while searching. As for the developer, your job is to optimize the content of a website to show it as a top result of a certain keyword. It's a known fact that 67% of Google users only look at the first five results, so we better make sure our content gets to it.
